Donation Ethics

The Department of Health and Human Services is considering an amendment to the National Organ Transplant Act that would effectively make it illegal to compensate donors of bone marrow stem cells collected through a process similar to blood donation, just as it is illegal to compensate for such cells collected directly from the bone marrow (through a much more invasive and painful procedure). My friend and colleague, Peter Jaworski, put together an open letter to the HHS arguing against this amendment, which I and several other ethicists signed. I helped edit the letter and put together the website.
